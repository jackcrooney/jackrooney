I wouldn't call it my favorite but definitely my most interesting drink experience, on my way back from a long road trip, we went to a coffee / drink resturaunt that isn't in San Diego, called the Dutch Bros. Here you get all sorts of fun and unique drinks, that you couldn't get anywhere else. Me and my sister Rosie decided to get the "churro freeze"... we have come to greatly regret this decision. A medium churro freeze (there is a large) has 800 calories, 250mg of caffeine, and over 120g of sugar. I have no idea how that is legal, but I was terrified of an imminent heart attack, because I foolishly drank the entire thing BEFORE checking the nutrional values. To say the least I am appaled, and the drink was DELICOUS.
